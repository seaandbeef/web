FAQ
Common questions.
Clear expectations for drops, shares, and fulfillment.
How do drops work? ▾
A drop opens for a limited time with a headline cut or seafood set and a total quantity (example: 500 lb). Members reserve shares. When the drop fills, we fulfill the group order and schedule pickup or delivery.
What if the drop doesn’t fill? ▾
If a drop doesn’t meet its minimum, it may be extended, adjusted, or canceled. If canceled, you’ll be refunded, depending on your payment flow.
Where do you source from? ▾
We curate top-tier beef and seafood suppliers per drop. Examples include elite Australian Wagyu farms and true Japanese A5 programs, plus premium seafood from the best countries. Every drop lists origin, grade, and handling notes.
Pickup vs delivery? ▾
Drops specify available fulfillment methods. Pickup includes a time window and location. Delivery includes a delivery zone and scheduled window, with cold-chain packaging.
Can I change my share after reserving? ▾
During the drop window, you can request changes depending on availability. After the drop closes, changes may be limited.
What about refunds and quality issues? ▾
If there’s an issue, contact us within 24 hours of delivery or pickup with photos and order details. Resolutions may include replacement, store credit, or refund depending on the situation and the drop.
